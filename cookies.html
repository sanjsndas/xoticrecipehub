Our Cookies & Tracking Philosophy

Just like a perfect blend of spices makes a dish memorable, cookies help us create a seamless, personalized experience on Xotic Recipe Hub. We use transparent, privacy-first tracking that never compromises your trust. Here's everything you need to know — plus how to adjust the heat level.

Cookies Policy effective: April 22, 2026 — Version 2.0 (Flavorful & Fair)

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They help remember your actions and preferences (like language, dark mode, or favorite spicy categories) over time, so you don't have to re-enter them on every visit. Think of them as helpful kitchen assistants — they remember where you left the chili flakes!

3. Third-Party Cookies & Integrated Services

Some features on Xotic Recipe Hub (embedded recipe videos from YouTube, social sharing buttons, or interactive spice maps) may set third-party cookies. These providers (e.g., YouTube, FontAwesome, Instagram) have their own privacy and cookie policies. We only use services that comply with data protection regulations. You can block third-party cookies through your browser settings, but some interactive features may be limited.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You are always in control. You can adjust your cookie settings in three easy ways:

  • Interactive Cookie Manager: Click the "Cookie Preferences" button below or the floating spice jar icon (bottom-right on most pages) to change your consent anytime.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via privacy settings. Check your browser's "Help" section for instructions (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics cookies by enabling “Do Not Track” in your browser or using our opt-out mechanism in the preference center.

5. Cookie Duration & Retention

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain for a defined period, up to 24 months). Persistent cookies are renewed each time you visit. You can manually delete all cookies via browser settings at any time. After deletion, some preferences will reset, and you may see the cookie banner again.

6. Your Consent & Legal Basis (GDPR / CCPA / ePrivacy)

For non-essential cookies, we always request your active consent before placing them on your device. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the functionality of essential services. Under CCPA, you can opt-out of any “sale” of data — but we do not sell personal information via cookies or any other means. Our analytics are aggregated and anonymized.

Legal & ethical foundation: Our cookie practices follow GDPR Article 7 (consent), ePrivacy Directive (Cookie Law), and California Consumer Privacy Act. We document all consents with timestamps to ensure accountability.

7. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” feature that signals websites you don’t want to be tracked. Xotic Recipe Hub honors DNT headers: if your browser sends a DNT:1 signal, we will disable analytics and functional tracking cookies by default, only serving essential cookies. No additional action needed.

8. Updates to This Cookies Policy

We may revise this policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or our data practices. Any significant updates will be communicated via a website banner or email (if you are subscribed). The "last updated" date at the top indicates the latest revision. Continued use of the website implies acceptance of the revised policy, but for new cookies categories we will re-ask for consent.
