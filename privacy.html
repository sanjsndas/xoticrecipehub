Privacy & Spice Protection

At Xotic Recipe Hub, we honor your personal data as much as we honor heirloom chili recipes. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control. Transparency is our secret ingredient.

Last updated: April 22, 2026 — Version 2.4 (Fiery Compliance)

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the most flavorful experience and improve our spicy content, we collect limited personal data in the following categories:

  • 🌶️ Identity Data Full name, username, or social handle — only when you voluntarily submit via contact forms, newsletter signups, or recipe contributions.
  • 🔥 Contact Data Email address, phone number (optional) — used to reply to inquiries, send recipe updates, or spicy alerts.
  • 🍅 Technical Data IP address, browser type, device info, and usage patterns — collected via cookies and analytics to optimize our site performance and red-themed UI.
  • 🌿 Communication Data Any message, recipe idea, or feedback you send through our contact form, social media DMs, or email correspondence.

We never intentionally collect sensitive personal data (like health or financial details) unless you explicitly provide it for collaboration purposes, and even then we treat it with extra confidentiality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data fuels our mission to build a global red-cuisine community. We use information for:

  • Responding to your inquiries — within 24-48 hours, we answer spice-related questions, recipe swaps, or collab proposals.
  • Newsletters & marketing — only if you opt-in. You can unsubscribe anytime via the “spice-free” link in emails.
  • Improving our content — aggregated analytics help us see which recipes and pages (like our fiery contact form or privacy hub) are most loved.
  • Legal & security — to prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
Spice promise: We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your email is safer than a sealed jar of ghost pepper powder.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Xotic Recipe Hub uses essential and analytical cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These small data files help remember your preferences (like dark/light mode) and count visitor traffic. We do not use invasive advertising cookies without consent. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. For full details, please visit our Cookie Policy page.

4. Sharing Your Data: Only When Necessary

We may share your information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website (e.g., email delivery platforms, analytics like Fathom or Google Analytics anonymized). All processors are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. Legal disclosure only occurs if required by law, to protect our rights, or in case of a merger/acquisition — you would be notified promptly.

5. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures: SSL encryption, firewalls, regular malware scans, and limited internal access. Your contact form submissions are stored in encrypted databases. We retain personal data only as long as necessary: up to 24 months for inactive contacts, or longer if you’re an active subscriber or have ongoing collaboration. You may request deletion anytime (see Section 7).

6. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA / Global)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — obtain a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”) — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability — receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object — opt-out of direct marketing or certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@xoticrecipehub.com or use our contact form with the subject “Privacy Request”. We will respond within 30 days (usually faster). No fee will be charged unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our spicy recipes are loved by all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise young chefs while browsing.

8. Third-Party Links & Social Features

Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., ingredient suppliers, YouTube cooking tutorials) and social media plugins. Xotic Recipe Hub is not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their policies before interacting.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States, but we welcome visitors from around the globe. If you access our site from the European Economic Area (EEA) or other regions with different data protection laws, please note that your information may be transferred to the US. We ensure appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As our red recipe community evolves, we may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date. Significant changes will be highlighted via website banner or email notification (if you’re subscribed). We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Your trust is our main ingredient. If you have any doubts or spicy questions about how we handle your data, please reach out to our Data Protection Officer (DPO) directly: dpo@xoticrecipehub.com or call our recipe hotline +1 (888) 357-9425 and ask for the privacy team.

11. How to Contact Us

For any privacy-specific concerns, data deletion requests, or general inquiries about this policy:

  • Email (Privacy dedicated): privacy@xoticrecipehub.com
  • Postal address: Xotic Recipe Hub – Privacy Office, 422 Red Spice Avenue, Culinary District, NYC 10014, USA
  • Contact form: Use our Get In Touch page and mark “Privacy / Data request” in subject.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CNIL in France) if you believe your privacy rights have been infringed. However, we’d appreciate the chance to resolve any issue first.
